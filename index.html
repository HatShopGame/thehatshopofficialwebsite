We are all still in lock down for Covid-19, things have began to pick back up and major strides have been made to make the game work well and an enjoyable experience. At this rate we hope to finish some time in the summer of 2021.

Covid-19 has been tough on us all and progress has stopped as we have been trying to adjust to what life in isolation will be like. We are hoping to begin working on the game again in the fall, however this may not be possible pending on the state of things. Stay safe!

With Covid-19 on the rise all of work and developemnet is happening online, this is a scarry time for many so we are all keeping safe and staying in quarantine. Now with all our free time from school and actvities we can do more work than ever before!

Loads of new art, textures and more have been added to the game and our developers are hard at work making it great. We don't know when we will launch to steam but we are hopping to finish our little pet project by 2022.

08/21/2019
The Game "The Hat Shop" begins, the game is an rpg about collecting different hats to power up your charecter and gain special ablities. You are transported to an unknown word from the hat shop where these hats hold power and are the keys to your succes. By defeating enemies along your journey and exploring the world, you will uncover the truth about about the new mysterious land.
